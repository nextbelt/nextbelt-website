Service Details

Deep Dive Into Each Service

01 — Preventive Maintenance

Catch Defects on a PM Round, Not on a Shutdown

We build PM rounds around your production calendar so inspections happen during planned windows, not after a failure. Each visit covers belt wear, splice integrity, lagging condition, bearing and roller health, and tracking alignment. You get a graded report with photos and prioritized findings — the inputs you need to plan repairs and shift your work from reactive to planned.

  • Scheduled visits aligned to your production calendar
  • Belt wear tracking with measurement records
  • Splice health inspection and grading
  • Bearing and roller condition monitoring
  • Tracking alignment verification
  • Detailed inspection reports with photos
02 — Emergency Repairs

Line Down? We Stabilize, Fix, and Find the Root Cause

When a belt fails, your throughput stops. Our crew handles tears, delamination, mistracking, drive failures, sensor faults, and structural damage. We arrive equipped to stabilize the line, make a vulcanized repair that holds, and run a root cause analysis — so the same defect doesn't put you down again next week.

  • Rapid deployment to your facility
  • Belt tear repair and vulcanized splicing
  • Drive and motor troubleshooting
  • Sensor and control system diagnostics
  • Structural component replacement
  • Root cause analysis for every incident
03 — Belt Drop-Shipping

The Right Belt, Delivered Fast

Sourcing conveyor belts shouldn't require weeks of back-and-forth. Through our supplier network, we source, spec, and ship belts directly to your dock. Whether you need a standard replacement or a specialty belt for extreme conditions, we match the right product to your requirements and handle the logistics.

  • Access to major belt manufacturers
  • Competitive pricing with volume discounts
  • Direct-to-dock shipping nationwide
  • Spec matching for your exact system
  • Expedited options for urgent needs
  • Inventory planning support
04 — Custom Belt Matching

Precision-Matched to Your System

No two conveyor lines load the same. We pull your load profile, belt speed, operating environment, and existing drive geometry, then spec the belt to fit: ply count, top cover compound, traction surface, tensile rating, and edge finish. The goal is straightforward — longer belt life, fewer mistracking events, and less time at the conveyor.

  • Load profile and environment analysis
  • Ply, cover, and tensile specification
  • Traction surface optimization
  • Temperature and chemical resistance matching
  • Edge type and cleating options
  • Competitive lead time sourcing
05 — Curved Conveyor Installation

Portec-Style Systems, Done Right

Curved belt installs live or die on alignment — a general contractor won't get the radius right. We work Portec-style curved systems end to end: strip the worn belt, set curve alignment and radius, match line speed to adjacent conveyors, and tune the hand-off points upstream and downstream. The payoff is clean package flow without the jams that pile up at a mistuned transfer.

  • Complete belt removal and installation
  • Curve alignment and radius calibration
  • Speed matching to adjacent conveyors
  • Hand-off point optimization
  • Post-install tracking verification
  • System integration testing
06 — Telescopic Conveyors

Extendable Systems Specialists

Telescopic conveyors from FMH, Caljan, and Extendo require specialized knowledge. We handle belt tensioning, tracking adjustment, splice alignment, and all safety checks. Whether it's a belt replacement, mechanical overhaul, or performance optimization, we understand the unique mechanics of extendable conveyor systems.

  • FMH, Caljan, Extendo expertise
  • Belt tensioning and tracking
  • Splice alignment and repair
  • Extension mechanism maintenance
  • Safety interlock verification
  • Performance optimization
