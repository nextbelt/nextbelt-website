The RMI Framework

We assess your organization across three critical dimensions to reveal structural factors limiting performance.

01 — People

Workforce Capability

We assess technician competency, knowledge transfer mechanisms, and cultural alignment with reliability goals.

02 — Process

Operational Discipline

We evaluate planning & scheduling discipline, PM effectiveness, and standard operating procedures (SOPs).

03 — Technology

Systems & Data

We analyze data integrity, ISO 14224 compliance, and how effectively your CMMS supports reliability.

Our Methodology

We don't just interview executives—we get on the floor with your technicians to find the ground truth.

01

Discovery

On-site interviews and job shadowing. We review actual maintenance practices in real-time.

02
