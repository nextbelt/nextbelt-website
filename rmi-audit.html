What is the Reliability Maturity Index?

The RMI is a standardized methodology for evaluating the maturity of maintenance and reliability programs. It helps organizations identify gaps, benchmark performance, and create targeted improvement roadmaps.

Objective Scoring

Quantitative assessment across 16 critical dimensions of maintenance excellence

Gap Identification

Pinpoint exactly where your organization needs improvement

Benchmarking

Compare your maturity against industry standards and best practices

Roadmap Creation

Generate actionable improvement plans based on assessment results

Three Pillars

The Three Pillars of RMI Assessment

People

Workforce capability and culture. Evaluates technician competency, knowledge transfer mechanisms, and cultural alignment with reliability goals.

  • Technician Competency
  • Training & Development
  • Knowledge Transfer
  • Cultural Alignment
  • Leadership & Accountability

Processes

Standardized procedures and workflows. Measures the maturity of your maintenance processes and their consistency across the organization.

  • Standard Operating Procedures
  • Root Cause Analysis
  • Failure Mode Analysis
  • Spare Parts Management
  • Vendor Management

Technology & Tools

Digital tools and systems enablement. Assesses your organization's adoption and effective use of maintenance technology and data analytics.

  • CMMS/EAM Utilization
  • Predictive Maintenance Tools
  • Data Quality & Analytics
  • Mobile Technology Adoption
  • Integration & Automation
