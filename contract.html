MASTER SERVICES & ASSET PROTECTION AGREEMENT

Effective Date: December 31, 2025

This Agreement is entered into as of December 31, 2025, by and between NextBelt LLC ("Client" or "Company"), an Arizona limited liability company, and Camilo A. Mateus ("Contractor" or "Assignor"), an individual residing in Colombia.

SECTION 1 — NON-DISCLOSURE AGREEMENT

Purpose

Receiving Party will access certain proprietary, confidential, and trade-secret information solely for performing development work for the NextBelt SMMS platform ("Purpose"). No other use is permitted.

Definition of Confidential Information

"Confidential Information" includes all non-public information disclosed by Company, including AI models, embeddings, SMMS logic, AI components, document/API extraction logic, API translation engine mappings, derivatives, outputs, and trade secrets.

Exclusions

Information shall not be considered Confidential only if Receiving Party proves with written evidence that it was publicly available without breach, rightfully received, independently developed without reference, or approved in signed writing.

Use Restrictions

Confidential Information may be used exclusively for the Purpose and shall not be reused, disclosed, reverse engineered, stored insecurely, or introduced into any public or external AI/LLM tool.

Reverse Engineering Ban

Receiving Party shall not reverse engineer, decompile, emulate, replicate, or derive competing logic from any portion of the SMMS platform.

Security Obligations

Contractor shall maintain full-disk encryption, strong credentials, multi-factor authentication, VPN usage on public networks, antivirus protection, and exclusive-use devices.

Non-Competition

During the engagement and for two (2) years thereafter, Contractor shall not directly or indirectly engage in, assist, or consult with any business that competes with the Company's SMMS platform in the following areas: (a) AI-powered computerized maintenance management systems (CMMS); (b) predictive maintenance or reliability analytics platforms utilizing machine learning; or (c) asset management software with AI-driven document extraction or API translation capabilities. This restriction applies to the markets in which Company actively operates or has documented plans to expand. Contractor acknowledges this scope is reasonable given Contractor's access to Company's core trade secrets and competitive strategies.

Trade Secret Survival

Confidentiality and trade secret obligations shall survive indefinitely, regardless of termination or expiration of this Agreement.

Enhanced Liquidated Damages

Any breach of confidentiality, IP, non-compete, or non-circumvention obligations shall trigger liquidated damages of no less than USD $250,000, acknowledged as a reasonable pre-estimate given Company IP value exceeding $1,000,000.

Injunctive Relief

Contractor acknowledges that breach would cause irreparable harm and Company may seek immediate injunctive relief without posting bond.

Governing Law

This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the State of Arizona, United States, without regard to its conflict of laws principles.

Arbitration (SIAC)

Any dispute shall be finally settled under the Rules of the Singapore International Arbitration Centre (SIAC) by one arbitrator. Seat: Singapore. Language: English. The arbitrator shall apply Arizona law as specified above.

Force Majeure

Neither party shall be liable for delays or failures in performance resulting from acts beyond their reasonable control, including but not limited to natural disasters, war, terrorism, government actions, internet or infrastructure outages, or pandemic. The affected party shall provide prompt notice and use reasonable efforts to mitigate the impact. If a force majeure event continues for more than thirty (30) days, either party may terminate this Agreement without penalty.

Agile Pivot & Demo Priority

SECTION 3 — REPOSITORY ACCESS & SECURITY POLICY

Permitted Access Methods

Repository access shall be granted exclusively via direct invite to NextBelt's private GitHub organization (github.com/nextbelt-llc) using Company-provided SSO and 2FA-enforced accounts. No cloning to personal machines is permitted unless the entire drive is encrypted (BitLocker, FileVault, VeraCrypt, or LUKS) and Contractor has provided a screenshot confirming full-disk encryption is active.

Mandatory SOC2 Oversight

Continuous screen recording software (e.g., Loom, Tango, or NextBelt-provided tool) must be running during all repository access and work sessions to satisfy SOC2 Type II and Vanta audit requirements.

Security Requirements

Contractor shall maintain: (a) up-to-date operating system and antivirus software; (b) full-disk encryption enabled at all times; (c) VPN connection when using public Wi-Fi; and (d) multi-factor authentication on all accounts with repository access.

Absolute Prohibitions (Zero Tolerance)

Contractor shall NOT, under any circumstances: (a) clone, fork, download, or store repository contents on any personal GitHub, GitLab, Bitbucket, or cloud drive; (b) take unauthorized screenshots or photographs of code; (c) copy any code snippet—even one line—into ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Cursor, local LLMs, or any AI tool other than the authorized GitHub Copilot; (d) store code on unencrypted, shared, or family devices; (e) discuss, describe, or disclose any aspect of the code, architecture, SMMS logic, AI components, document/API extraction logic, or prompts with any third party, including forums or private groups; or (f) use any portion of the code (even anonymized) in portfolios, blogs, LinkedIn, GitHub profiles, or job applications.

Authorized AI Exception

Organization-issued GitHub Copilot is the only approved AI coding assistant. Use of any other AI tool for code-related work requires explicit prior written authorization from Company.

Monitoring & Audit Rights

Company reserves the right to: (a) require periodic screenshots proving full-disk encryption is active; (b) require live screen-share audits with 24 hours' notice; (c) audit Contractor systems, logs, repositories, and recordings for compliance verification; and (d) revoke access instantly and without notice if any violation is suspected.
