What We Do

Comprehensive belt services backed by 30+ years of field experience.

01 — Preventive

Maintenance & Inspections

Scheduled visits for wear tracking, lagging checks, bearings, tracking, and splice health to extend belt life.

02 — Emergency

Repairs & Troubleshooting

Rapid response for tears, mistracking, drive failures, and sensor issues. We stabilize the line and get you running.

03 — Logistics

Nationwide Belt Drop-Shipping

Belts shipped direct from our network. We'll spec, quote, and arrange delivery to your dock fast.

04 — Sourcing

Custom Belt Matching

We match ply, top cover, traction, and tensile requirements to your load and environment, then source at competitive lead times.

05 — Curved

Portec-style Installation

Complete removal and installation with curve alignment, speed match, and hand-off tuning to upstream/downstream equipment.

Extendable Conveyors

FMH, Caljan, Extendo. We handle tensioning, tracking, splice alignment, and safety checks to minimize downtime.
