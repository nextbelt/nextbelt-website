Reliability Maturity Index

Escape the "Reactive Spiral"

70% of maintenance teams run reactive — wrench time burned on breakdowns, not defect elimination. The Reliability Maturity Index (RMI) scores exactly how far you sit from a planned, predictive operation, and what's standing in the way.

See Engagement Tiers

The RMI Framework

We score your operation across People, Process, and Technology — the three pillars that decide whether reliability sticks or slides back into reactive.

01 — People

Workforce Capability

We gauge technician skill depth, how tribal knowledge gets captured and handed off, and whether the floor actually owns reliability — or just chases the next breakdown.

02 — Process

Operational Discipline

We test your planning & scheduling discipline, planned-vs-reactive ratio, whether PMs are catching defects or just burning labor, and how well RCA closes the loop on repeat failures.

03 — Technology

Systems & Data

We check whether your failure data is clean and ISO 14224-aligned, and whether your CMMS is actually driving PdM decisions — or just logging work orders after the fact.

Our Methodology

We don't run on conference-room interviews. We get on the floor, ride along on work orders, and pull the real data — because the spreadsheet and the asset rarely tell the same story.

01

Discovery

On-site interviews and job shadowing. We watch how work really gets planned, executed, and closed out — not how the binder says it should.

02

Analysis

We mine work order history for failure patterns and bad-actor assets, check MRO/spares accuracy, and surface the defects driving your unplanned downtime.

03

The Scorecard

You get a Reliability Maturity Score (1-5) per pillar, with the specific gaps holding each one back — no vague letter grade, an actual diagnosis.

04

The Roadmap

A prioritized roadmap — sequenced and resourced — to shift wrench time from reactive firefighting toward planned, predictive maintenance.
