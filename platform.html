How SMMS Works

From asset registry to defect elimination, here's how SMMS moves your program from reactive to planned.

01

Connect Your Assets

Integrate sensors, import equipment data, or start fresh. We'll help you build a complete asset registry.

02

Set Up Workflows

Configure PM schedules, approval chains, and notification rules that match your operations.

03

Monitor & Predict

Condition data and trends flag developing faults early, so you intervene during a planned window instead of at 2 a.m.
